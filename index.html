Crystal Ball Magic Addition Game


How to Play

  • At the start of each game, you will be given a Magic Number that you need to match exactly by determining the mystery values of each crystal ball.
  • By clicking on one of the four crystal balls below, you will increase your sum by a mystery amount between 1 and 12 that you learn by clicking on that crystal ball.
  • To win the game, match your sum to the Magic Number without going over.
  • You will receive a new Magic Number each time you start a new game.
  • The crystal balls will hold a new value as well!
  • Good luck!
